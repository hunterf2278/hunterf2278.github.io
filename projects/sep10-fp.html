SEP10 Freedom Project

Context:

We were assingned a year long project about a topic of our choice. For example, I picked the topic writing because I am really passionate about it and their is backgorund info about it. Then we had to pick a tool, such as the 3d model tool Aframe. Then use that to make a product or website, any technology that can improve work in this topic. Finally, we had to create our own website using two bootstrap components that we klearned, an then present our website and product to judges at the Expo.

Content:

My product was called the Screen Pen, which is an electric pen with an eraser and a screen. While people write, the screen shows the sentence, then underlines the word and flahses red to indicate that the student made a grammatical error. It flashes blue to tell the perosn they mispelled a word. The Screen pen only shows you the error, the student has to figure out why its wrong and whats the correct answer. This way, students are forced to use their brains and solve the porblem themselves. Instead, of using chatgpt here they get the answer and don't learn anything from it.
