SEP10 Freedom Project

Context:

We were assingned a year long project about a topic of our choice. For example, I picked the topic writing because I am really passionate about it and their is backgorund info about it. Then we had to pick a tool, such as the 3d model tool Aframe. Then use that to make a product or website, any technology that can improve work in this topic. Finally, we had to create our own website using two bootstrap components that we klearned, an then present our website and product to judges at the Expo.

Content:

My product was called the Screen Pen, which is an electric pen with an eraser and a screen. While people write, the screen shows the sentence, then underlines the word and flahses red to indicate that the student made a grammatical error. It flashes blue to tell the perosn they mispelled a word. The Screen pen only shows you the error, the student has to figure out why its wrong and whats the correct answer. This way, students are forced to use their brains and solve the porblem themselves. Instead, of using chatgpt here they get the answer and don't learn anything from it.

Reflection:

One challenge I had was that the cards in my website weren't horizontally aligned. The problem was that my cards were individually wrapped in a container, then I used the Bootstrap columns and rows to align them each. So, after askign a fined for help and doing my own reserach, I learned that i needed to seperatge my cards. Then have them all wrapped in the same row and container. This was succesful and now my cards were aligned horizontally and loooekd very neat on my website.

Another challenge I had was using Aframe to build my 3d model of the Screen Pen. The position numbers were small and a bit confusing, this made it difficult to stack my shapes on top so they looked like a pen. So, I had to tinker aorund with the numbers and see what numbers made the shapes move up and down or left and right. It took a lot of time and focus to figure out the numbers, but it payed off becausse I succeded and finished my 3d model.

If I had more time I would edit the spaces in between my cards on the first page of my website, so it looks cleaner. I would also change the color scheme of my website.
